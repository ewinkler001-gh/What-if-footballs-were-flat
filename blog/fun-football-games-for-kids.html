Fun Football Games for Kids (Easy Backyard Games Everyone Will Love)

Football games are a great way to help kids stay active, build confidence, and have fun outdoors. Whether you're practicing skills or just looking for backyard entertainment, these easy football games are perfect for young players.

Why Football Games Are Great for Kids

Simple football activities help kids:

  • Improve coordination
  • Build teamwork skills
  • Develop confidence
  • Stay active and engaged

The best part is that many of these games can be played almost anywhere.

1. Target Toss

Set up buckets, cones, or boxes and have kids try to throw the football into the targets.

This game improves throwing accuracy and hand-eye coordination.

2. Flag Pull Challenge

Kids wear flag belts while trying to avoid defenders who attempt to pull the flags.

This game helps improve agility and quick movement.

3. Quarterback Accuracy Game

Players earn points by hitting specific targets with their throws.

You can increase difficulty by moving farther away from the target.

Want to improve technique first? Read our guide on how to teach kids to throw a football.

4. Obstacle Course Relay

Create a football-themed obstacle course using cones and markers.

Kids can run, dodge, throw, and catch while racing through the course.

5. Touchdown Dance Contest

After scoring, kids create funny touchdown celebrations.

This keeps football lighthearted and fun for younger players.

6. Two-Hand Touch Football

A simple backyard version of football that focuses on movement and teamwork without tackling.

If your child is new to the sport, you may also want to read our comparison of flag football vs tackle football.

7. Long Throw Challenge

Players compete to see who can throw the football the farthest while maintaining accuracy.

Using the correct football size makes this easier for young players.
