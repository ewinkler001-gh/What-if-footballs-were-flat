What If Footballs Were Flat?

A Funny Football Children's Book by Eric Winkler

A Funny Football Book for Kids Who Love Sports

What would happen if footballs suddenly went flat? Would the fullback fumble? Would fierce football players flop and fall trying to chase a floppy ball?

What If Footballs Were Flat? is a playful, imaginative children's football book packed with alliteration, humor, and sports fun. Young readers who love football and silly storytelling will laugh as the game changes in the most unexpected way.
